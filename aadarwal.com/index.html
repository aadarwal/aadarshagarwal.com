I am a sophomore at the University of Chicago studying physics and philosophy. aadarsh@uchicago.edu / @aadarwal on github, X, YT

As someone who has studied fundamental fields, I have had a few fundamental interests develop which have made me interested in a bunch of problems for which I hope to find a plan of attack:

  • Compute: Integrated Photonic, Quantum, Neuromorphic, these are some of the coolest things. This is effectively the ultimate mind and matter problem, and this is the kind of stuff that needs to be focused on for a while. memQ and High Lab are the places that I worked at and made me align interests along this path.
  • Propulsion: I think this is fundamentally going to change everything and all of human kinds lives. Please invest in this stuff. I spent my freshman summer at Skyroot Aerospace which is one of the main reasons I strongly feel there is an urgent need for this kind of work.
  • Social Contract: Philosophy in general
  • India: If there is a general way to be able to apply as much change as is needed

A lot of this was fundamentally based on the version of the future that I think could be created, but I really do hope that it is much weirder than we can possibly imagine.
